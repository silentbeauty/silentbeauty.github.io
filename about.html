So, who is Silentbeauty?

Hello all, I'm Erin aka Silentbeauty, an original member of the GEEK CHIC tribe! Not only do I still have my GEEK CHIC t-shirts from when I attended the summer program at Oregon State University, I actually returned to work at GEEK CHIC a couple years later. If that doesn't grant me permanent GEEK GIRL status, I don't know what should.

These days I'm a blogger, a NUS hack volunteer, an American ex-pat, a photographer, a coder, and the best Wife & Mom that I know how to be!

Currently I'm brushing up on my tech skills with an eye towards launching a career as a freelance web designer & developer who specialises in WordPress devleopment. Need some help on a project, tweet me!
